Applications

Where It Secures

01
Pallet Wrapping
The primary application — unitizing palletized goods for shipping. Prevents shifting, toppling, and product damage in transit.
02
Transport Security
Stabilizes loads during truck, rail, and sea freight. Reduces damage claims and returns from shifting cargo.
03
Dust & Moisture Shield
Protects stored inventory from dust, dirt, and light moisture. Keeps products clean from warehouse to customer.
04
Product Bundling
Groups multiple items together for handling efficiency. Replaces strapping and banding for lighter loads.
05
Warehouse Storage
Wraps racked inventory to prevent dust accumulation and package deterioration during long-term storage.

Tamper Evidence
Stretch wrapping makes tampering obvious. Any cut or tear in the film indicates potential interference.

Why Our Stretch

What Makes It Superior

Not all stretch film delivers the same containment. Our LLDPE formula is optimized for maximum stretch with consistent cling.

01

Maximum Stretch Memory

Stretches up to 300% and maintains tension. The film tries to return to original size, creating constant holding force on your load.

02

One-Side Cling

Clings to itself but not to products or pallets. Loads slide off cleanly without sticking or leaving residue.

03

Superior Puncture Resistance

Multi-layer cast film construction resists tears and punctures from sharp pallet corners or product edges.

04

Clear Optical Clarity

High-clarity film lets you scan barcodes and inspect products through the wrap without removing it.

05

Cost Per Load Optimized

High stretch means less film per pallet. Stretch more, use less, save money on your packaging costs.
