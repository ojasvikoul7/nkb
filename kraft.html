Why This Tape

What Makes It Different

Kraft paper tape offers unique advantages that plastic tapes simply cannot match.

01

Eco-Friendly & Sustainable

Made from renewable wood pulp with natural rubber adhesive. Fully biodegradable and recyclable with cardboard packaging.

02

Superior Box Bonding

Unlike plastic tapes that sit on the surface, Kraft tape fibers bond with cardboard, becoming part of the box structure.

03

Tamper-Evident Security

Any attempt to remove the tape damages the box surface, providing clear visual evidence of tampering.

04

Writeable Surface

The matte paper surface accepts pen, pencil, and marker. Perfect for labeling, dating, or adding personal messages.

05

Professional Aesthetic

The natural brown color and texture communicate quality and environmental consciousness to your customers.
