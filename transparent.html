Why Clear Tape

What Makes It Superior

Not all clear tapes are truly transparent. Our crystal-clear BOPP delivers optical clarity with industrial strength.

01

True Optical Clarity

90%+ light transmission with no yellowing. Your packaging looks clean and professional, not cloudy or aged.

02

UV Resistant

Won't yellow or degrade under warehouse lighting or sunlight exposure. Stays crystal clear for the full shelf life.

03

Silent Unwind

Special coating reduces noise during application. Perfect for retail environments and high-volume packing stations.

04

Print Compatibility

Works with inline printers for custom messaging. The clear base makes your printed text pop.

05

Strong Initial Tack

Hot-melt adhesive grips instantly on contact. No sliding, no repositioning needed — place it once.
