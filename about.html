About Us

NKB Packaging Solutions provides quality packaging materials to businesses across India. We offer a range of products including BOPP tapes, stretch films, bubble pouches, foam rolls, and biodegradable bags.

Our focus is on delivering reliable packaging solutions that meet your business needs. We work with manufacturers, e-commerce companies, and retailers to ensure their products are well-protected during storage and transit.

Based in Ghaziabad, Uttar Pradesh, we serve customers throughout the country with timely delivery and consistent product quality.
